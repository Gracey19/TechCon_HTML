History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots gathering of developers and innovators. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform for sharing ideas, launching technologies, and building community.

Mission

TechCon’s mission is to empower creators, connect communities, and spark innovation through inclusive, accessible, and forward-thinking events.

Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted some of the most influential voices in tech:

  • Ada Lovelace – Pioneer of computing theory and algorithmic design.
  • Grace Hopper – Inventor of the first compiler and advocate for accessible programming.
  • Tim Berners-Lee – Creator of the World Wide Web and champion of open standards.
